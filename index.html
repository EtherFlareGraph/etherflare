Insure your website uptime!

What is it?

Unlike the classic Anti-DDoS services, that charge fixed monthly fee for their services with possible moneyback in case of fail. We provide you DDoS protection with Uptime Insurance as incentive, powered by Chainlink Oracle and Ethereum smart contracts. 

How it works?

You register your website on EtherFlare, and set DNS A-records to EtherFlare Anti-DDoS proxy server. After that Insurance Contract is created.

You send an amount of ether to Insurance Contract, this amount depends on EtherFlare insurance rate and your own downtime risks. Use calculator below, to find the best option. Insurance Contract issue to you DoS Token with the name (yourwebsite.com DoS Token) , that you can easly exchange back to Ether, in case you want to suspend the insurance.

While the service is active, the ChainLink Oracle system monitors your website uptime. If uptime is ok, then the Exchange Rate  for DoS Token decreases, so the token gradually looses its value in order for our company to make profit. But when downtime gets higher than Maximal Acceptable, the Exchange Rate for DoS Token goes to the moon up in proportion to the percentage of downtime, so the value of DoS Tokens you hold grows, in order to compensate for your downtime losses.
